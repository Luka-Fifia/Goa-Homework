Antelope

An antelope is a member of a diverse group of herbivorous mammals belonging to the family Bovidae, which includes gazelles, impalas, and elands. They are found primarily in Africa and parts of Asia. Antelopes are known for their slender bodies, long legs, and impressive agility, which help them evade predators.

  • Habitat: Grasslands, savannas, forests, and deserts.
  • Diet: Herbivores, feeding mainly on grasses, leaves, and shrubs.
